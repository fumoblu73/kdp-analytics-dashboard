Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
Important: This KDP Analytics Dashboard is a personal tool for managing your own Amazon KDP and advertising data. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

1. Information We Collect

Our KDP Analytics Dashboard collects and processes the following information:

  • Amazon KDP Data: Sales reports, royalty information, KENP reads, and book performance metrics
  • Amazon Ads Data: Campaign performance, ad spend, clicks, impressions, and conversion data
  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted authentication credentials
  • Usage Data: Sync timestamps, dashboard interactions, and system logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Providing analytics and reporting on your KDP and advertising performance
  • Synchronizing data between Amazon platforms and your personal dashboard
  • Generating insights and visualizations of your publishing business
  • Maintaining and improving the dashboard functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your Data Stays Private:
  • All data is stored securely on cloud servers with encryption
  • No data is transmitted to third-party services
  • Encryption is used for sensitive authentication data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone. This is a personal analytics tool:

  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
  • No advertising networks have access to your information
  • No analytics companies receive your data
  • Amazon receives only standard API requests for your own data

5. Amazon API Usage

Our dashboard integrates with Amazon services through official APIs:

  • Amazon KDP: We access your sales and royalty data via session cookies
  • Amazon Advertising API: We use OAuth 2.0 for secure access to your ad data
  • All API usage complies with Amazon's Terms of Service
  • We only request data that you explicitly authorize

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage for:

  • Maintaining your dashboard login session
  • Storing user preferences and settings
  • Caching data for improved performance
  • Enabling auto-sync functionality

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained:

  • Active Data: Until you delete it or stop using the dashboard
  • Sync Logs: Last 1,000 entries (automatically cleaned)
  • Authentication Tokens: Until expiry or manual revocation
  • Historical Data: Preserved for trend analysis (securely stored)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored about you
  • Delete: Remove any or all data from the system
  • Export: Download your data in JSON format
  • Revoke: Disconnect Amazon accounts at any time

9. Browser Extension Privacy

Our browser extension:

  • Does not track your browsing on other websites
  • Stores minimal data locally for functionality
  • Can be uninstalled at any time
